How to add your inputs

Add new daily data using a GitHub issue. The repository workflow will update data/checklist.json for you.

  1. Open a new issue in the repository.
  2. Select the Daily health entry template.
  3. Fill in the values and submit the issue.

Example issue body:

Date: 2026-05-31
Protein %: 90
Water glasses: 8
Exercise minutes: 30
Sleep hours: 7.5
General health: yes
Notes: Strong energy, good protein intake, stayed on track.

After the workflow runs, refresh the page to see your updated metrics.
